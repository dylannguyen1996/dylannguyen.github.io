The Story

The story of the self-driving car rental management project revolves around the pursuit of revolutionizing mobility. It begins with the vision of making autonomous vehicles accessible to the masses, addressing urban congestion and environmental concerns. Through cutting-edge technology and innovative solutions, the project aims to redefine how people move, offering a glimpse into the future of transportation.

The Challenge

The significant challenge in the self-driving car rental management project is efficiently integrating & overseeing a diverse fleet of vehicles, ensuring their safety and continuous operation, while also providing users with a smooth & user-friendly experience in booking.
